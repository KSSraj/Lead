About KASCC

Karnatak Arts, Science and Commerce College, Bidar is one among the 26 educational institutions being run by Karnataka Rashtriya Education Society Bidar. Established in 1970, the college is one of the prime Institutions of Bidar district. It is the biggest learning hub of Bidar district located at the Northern tip of Karnataka State. Although a number of colleges have come up lately in different parts of the district, yet this institution has been drawing the students from every corner of the state due to its ability to offer a spectrum of courses that are not available in newly established colleges. It attracts the students irrespective of gender, geographical location, social and economic strata.
